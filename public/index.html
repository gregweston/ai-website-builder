How to build your webpage

  1. 🤔 Think of an idea for a webpage — a game, a pet page, a story, anything you like!
  2. 💬 Tell the AI what to build, like "Make a page about dinosaurs with a fun fact quiz."
  3. ✨ Keep chatting to make it better — ask for new colors, sections, or features.
  4. 🖼️ Ask it to find pictures for your page, like "add a picture of a rocket."
  5. 🎨 It can also draw 2D and 3D shapes and animations — try "add a spinning cube" or "draw a bouncing ball."

Have fun and be creative! 🎉
